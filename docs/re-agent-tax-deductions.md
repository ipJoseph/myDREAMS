# Real Estate Agent Tax Deductions (Solopreneur LLC, Schedule C)

Reference guide for a single-member LLC real estate agent filing Schedule C.
Last updated: March 2026.

## Key Tax Facts (2026)

- Self-employment tax: 15.3% (12.4% SS + 2.9% Medicare) on 92.35% of net earnings
- SS wage base: $184,500 (2026)
- Additional Medicare tax: 0.9% on net earnings over $200k single / $250k joint
- Standard mileage rate: $0.725/mile
- Every $1 of deductions saves ~$0.14 in SE tax alone, plus your income tax rate
- The average agent overpays $3,000-$8,000/year by missing deductions

## Schedule C Line 9: Car and Truck Expenses

| Deduction | Notes |
|-----------|-------|
| Business mileage at $0.725/mile (2026) | Must keep contemporaneous log: date, destination, purpose, miles |
| OR actual vehicle expenses (gas, repairs, insurance, depreciation) | Prorate by business-use percentage; cannot switch methods on same vehicle |
| Parking fees and tolls | Deductible separately regardless of method |
| Car washes (business portion) | If meeting clients |

**Important:** Commuting from home to your brokerage office is NOT deductible. But if your home office qualifies, all drives from home to client meetings, showings, inspections are business miles.

## Schedule C Line 15: Insurance

| Deduction | Notes |
|-----------|-------|
| Errors & Omissions (E&O) insurance | Required by most brokerages |

**Note:** Health, dental, and vision insurance premiums are deducted on Form 1040 Line 17 (above-the-line), NOT Schedule C. 100% deductible if self-employed and not eligible for a spouse's employer plan.

## Schedule C Line 24b: Meals (50% Deductible)

| Deduction | Notes |
|-----------|-------|
| Client meals | Must discuss business; log who, where, purpose |
| Meals during business travel | Away from tax home overnight |
| Team meeting meals | If you're buying for others |

**Important:** Only 50% of meal costs are deductible. Must document: date, amount, who was present, business purpose.

## Schedule C Line 27a: Other Expenses

| Deduction | Notes |
|-----------|-------|
| Continuing education / CE courses | Required for license renewal |
| Coaching and mentorship programs | Business coaching (KW MAPS, etc.) |
| Books, podcasts, training materials | Business-related only |
| Client gifts | Limited to $25 per person per year |
| Closing gifts | Same $25/person limit |
| Home staging costs | If agent-paid |
| Professional attire/uniforms | Only if branded/logoed and not suitable for everyday wear |
| Bank fees on business accounts | Monthly service charges |
| Credit card processing fees | If you accept card payments |

## Schedule C Line 30: Business Use of Home (Form 8829)

| Method | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Simplified** | $5/sq ft, max 300 sq ft = $1,500 max |
| **Actual expense** | Proportional share of: mortgage interest/rent, property taxes, utilities, insurance, repairs, depreciation |

**Requirements:** Space must be used regularly AND exclusively for business, and must be your principal place of business OR where you regularly meet clients. Cannot claim both desk fees and home office.

## Above-the-Line Deductions (Form 1040, NOT Schedule C)

| Line | Deduction | Notes |
|------|-----------|-------|
| Schedule 1, Line 15 | 50% of self-employment tax | Automatic deduction |
| Schedule 1, Line 16 | SEP-IRA contributions | Up to 25% of net SE income (max ~$69,000 for 2026) |
| Schedule 1, Line 16 | Solo 401(k) contributions | Employee + employer portions; higher limits than SEP |
| Form 1040, Line 17 | Health/dental/vision premiums | 100% deductible if not eligible for employer plan |

## Top 10 Commonly Missed Deductions

1. **Brokerage desk fees** (often thousands/year)
2. **E&O insurance premiums**
3. **MLS fees and lockbox costs**
4. **Business portion of cell phone** (most agents use personal phone 80%+ for business)
5. **Health insurance premiums** (above-the-line, not Schedule C)
6. **Retirement contributions** (SEP-IRA can shelter 25% of net income)
7. **Home office deduction** (agents who work from home frequently skip this)
8. **Client gifts** ($25/person limit adds up across dozens of clients)
9. **Referral fees paid to other agents**
10. **Professional development** (coaching, conferences, CE beyond minimum)

## Record-Keeping Requirements

| Category | What to Keep |
|----------|-------------|
| Mileage | Date, destination, business purpose, miles driven (contemporaneous log) |
| Meals | Date, amount, who was present, business purpose |
| Gifts | Recipient name, date, amount, business relationship |
| Receipts | Required for any expense over $75; required for all meals regardless of amount |
| Home office | Square footage calculation, percentage of home used |
